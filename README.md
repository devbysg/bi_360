# AtliQ Hardware – Business Insights 360 | Power BI Project

## Overview

Business Insights 360 is an end-to-end Power BI analytics solution developed for AtliQ Hardware, a fictional consumer electronics company operating across multiple countries. The project was designed to transform traditional Excel-based reporting into a centralized, interactive business intelligence platform, enabling faster and more informed decision-making across departments.

## Business Challenge

As AtliQ Hardware expanded globally, its reliance on Excel for reporting and analysis created challenges such as fragmented data, slow reporting cycles, and limited visibility into business performance. These limitations contributed to poor strategic decisions and significant losses in the Latin American market.

To overcome these challenges, a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution was developed using Power BI, providing leadership teams with real-time insights across Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive functions.
